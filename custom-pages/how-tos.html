Using the Forum's

The forums on the portal is the best place to interact with the CIA team, ask questions and get responses on the timely manner. The CIA team is available for chat from time to time on the portal. You have access to the forums once you are a registered user on the portal. The portal has high level categories based on interests. Specific topics can be viewed in the each of the categories, which should provide information on the latest topics of discussion.

  • Categories provide a logical separation of the different topics that would interest you.
  • You can subscribe to rss feeds for any category that you might want to follow.
  • If you want to direct a question to a specific member of the portal, you do '@' and the person's username and name will appear for selection. This notifies the person and brings their attention to your post. If you have a question for the cia-team you can use '@cia-team' and the group is notified.
