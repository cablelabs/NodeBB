The Project

The Cable Information Architecture (CIA) project is a cable industry led initiative to accelerate information exchange from the back office to delivery platforms.

Through collaboration with the industry and CableLabs® members, the CIA project promotes:

  • Improved Information Flow

  • Common Vocabulary

  • Standard APIs Access

  • Rapid Transformation

  • Improved Data Quality & Integrity

Resulting in:

  • Accelerated Products to Market

  • Improved Customer Experience

  • Operational Efficiency
