The Project

The Cable Information Architecture (CIA) project is a cable industry led initiative to accelerate information exchange from the back office to delivery platforms.

Through collaboration with the industry and CableLabs® members, the CIA project promotes:

  • Improved Information Flow

  • Common Vocabulary

  • Standard APIs Access

  • Rapid Transformation

  • Improved Data Quality & Integrity

Resulting in:

  • Accelerated Products to Market

  • Improved Customer Experience

  • Operational Efficiency

The Foundation

The Data Model

The CIA solution starts with a comprehensive data model based on broader communication industry standards already in place.

Extending the Model

Through work with participating members, custom extensions fill in the gaps left by other information models and meet the specific needs of the cable industry and of individual members.

Standard APIs

The data model is complemented with standard APIs to create access and improve the flow of data.

The Focus

Zero-Touch Fulfillment

Improve/automate data flow for the order-to-activation process by targeting key interface points between B/OSS and provisioning platforms.

Unified Customer Data

Enable a unified view of customer data for use in self-service portals, operational analytics, and e-bonding.

Operational Analytics

The data model is complemented with standard APIs to create access and improve the flow of data.

Integrated Inventory

Provide a single, integrated view of enterprise Services and Resources for consumption by upstream B/OSS platforms.

Location Services

Unify location data through a standard API for usage across the B/OSS, with a specific focus on serviceability and enabling location-based services

The Difference

Industry Insight

The CIA project works directly with participating MSO members, and is the only effort to provide the cable industry with a common architecture and the tools and resources to fully support its implementation.

Extensive Toolset

The project provides an interface library, model-driven API payload definitions, user stories, online documentation, and reference implementations.

By Developers for Developers

The CIA data model is created by developers for developers, with a focus on providing an Agile environment to promote speed of deployment.

About CableLabs

CableLabs is a non-profit research and development consortium that is dedicated to creating innovative ideas that significantly impact our cable operator members’ business. CableLabs also serves to define interoperable solutions among our members and their technology suppliers in order to drive scale, reduce costs, and create competition in the supply chain. CableLabs membership is comprised of the major cable operators worldwide.
